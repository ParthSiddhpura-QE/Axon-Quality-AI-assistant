Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Quality Engineering Assistant, available as a Microsoft Teams app, Google Workspace Add-on, Slack app, and ChatGPT Custom GPT (collectively, the "Service"). The Service is provided by Quality-Engineering-Skills (github.com/RBraga01/Quality-Engineering-Skills), operated by Ricardo Romão Marques Braga.

What data we process

When you use the Service, the text you type (your quality engineering questions, 8D descriptions, FMEA content, NCR observations, audit notes) is sent to an AI provider to generate a response. The AI providers used are:

  • Anthropic Claude — used by the Slack bot
  • Google Gemini — used by the Google Workspace Add-on
  • Microsoft Copilot — used by the Teams app
  • OpenAI GPT — used by the ChatGPT Custom GPT

What we do NOT do

  • We do not store your text inputs or the AI responses.
  • We do not log your queries or build user profiles.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetise your data.
  • We do not use your data to train AI models.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or workplace identifiers.

AI provider data handling

Text you send through the Service is processed by the respective AI provider under their own privacy policies and terms of service. We recommend reviewing these before using the Service with sensitive or confidential information:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Microsoft Privacy Statement
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

Sensitive information

Do not submit trade secrets, personally identifiable information (PII), or information classified as confidential under your organisation's data policies through the Service. Use general descriptions of problems rather than proprietary part numbers, customer names, or internal references where your organisation's policy requires it.

Open source

Quality-Engineering-Skills is open source (MIT licence). The full source code, including all skill definitions and platform connectors, is available at github.com/RBraga01/Quality-Engineering-Skills .

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to report a concern, open an issue at github.com/RBraga01/Quality-Engineering-Skills/issues .
